About Accessibility Features

Auto-Contrast: When enabled, the system automatically calculates and applies text colors that meet WCAG contrast standards for all trait chips and interface elements.
High Contrast Mode: Enhances visual contrast throughout the interface to improve readability for users with vision difficulties.
Reduced Motion: Minimizes or disables animations and transitions when your system preferences indicate motion sensitivity.
Contrast Levels:
  • AA: Standard accessibility (4.5:1 contrast ratio) - recommended for most users
  • AAA: Enhanced accessibility (7:1 contrast ratio) - for users with high accessibility needs
